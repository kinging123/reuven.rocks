A bit about myself

I'm Reuven Karasik. I'm currently 18 years old.

I started writing code when I was 7. Since then, I taught myself some useful things, including both Front-End and Back-End Web Development. I've been a freelance developer since the age of 13 and worked at a few small startups in Jerusalem while finishing High School.
In 2015 I founded the Tomorrow JLM community for young entrepreneurs in Jerusalem, and organized a couple of large Hackathons with over 100 participants.

I currently work at AlefAlefAlef, a Hebrew Typography Studio, and make an effort to travel and have fun in my free time.
